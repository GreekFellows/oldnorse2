Introduction.

This is a dictionary and database for the extinct language Old Norse (precisely speaking, this is for Old Icelandic, a dialect of Old Norse).

Look up the entry for an Old Norse word by typing the word in the search box above and click Go. Specify the part-of-speech of the word to look up in the dropdown list Search.... Choose From English to search for appearances of an English word in definitions.

Click Wordbook for a guide to understanding the entry format. Click Guide for an explanation on Old Norse declension, conjugation, grammar, syntax, pronunciation and more about the language itself.
